Elephant and Tailor

Elephants and tailors Once upon a time, a tailor was making clothes for his son in his shop. Suddenly a baby elephant came. And suddenly he began to speak loudly. The tailor gave him a piece of bread. She always came to the tailor shop. One day the elephant came but the tailor ran away with the needle instead of bread. The next day the elephant came back but its trunk was full of mud. And he threw mud from his trunk on the tailor's clothes. All the clothes of the tailor were ruined. The tailor grabbed his head and started crying. We learn a lesson from this story that you will do as you please.
