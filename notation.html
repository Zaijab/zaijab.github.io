1. Filling in The Blanks

There is a trade off between rigor and clarity. If one gives too many details, the overall picture is obfuscated. At times, the notation in these posts will rely on filling in the proper variables with the right 'type' to make the formulae have any meaning. What this accomplishes in my notes is a form of multiple dispatch.
