2.1. Sleep

Sleep 09:30PM - 06:00AM

2.1.1. Theory

  1. Why do we sleep

    The desire to sleep is caused by two main forces, adenosine buildup and circadian cycles.

  2. Adenosine and Caffine

    Adenosine is a chemical waste product. Adenosine is produced by cells expending energy. Adenosine buildup causes tiredness. Caffine blocks adenosine receptors, halting the tiredness. However adenosine builds up naturally causing a crash after caffine wears off.

  3. Circadian Cycles and Light

    Upon waking, a cortisol pulse happens causing alertness and starting a timer which will release melatonin. These pulses happen all the time, however they become off sync with the 24hr clock. To syncronise these pulses, view low solar angle sunlight at sunrise and sunset.

    Sunrise

    Sunset helps remove the bad effect of light late at night.

  4. Exercise and Food Intake

    Exercise helps shift the clock earlier in the day, helping one wake up earlier.
