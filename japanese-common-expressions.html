9. Giving and Recieving

Three words,

Give あげる あげた
Take もらう もらった
Given (to me) くらる くらた

Standard rules apply:

  • Use が for the subject
  • Use を for the object
  • Use に for the indirect object

Make sure to talk about the subject first for あげる and もらる. If I gave a cake to my mother, I could say:

  1. I gave a cake to my mother.
  2. My mother received a cake from me.

The latter sentence is awkward and would not be used.
