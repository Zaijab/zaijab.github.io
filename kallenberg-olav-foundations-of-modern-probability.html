2. Chapter 1 Sets and Functions, Measures and Integration

Introduces the foundational structures such as (systems closed under intersections), λ-systems (systems closed under limits), alongside their links using the Monotone Class Theorem, that if a π-system is contained in a λ-system then the smallest σ-algebra containing the π-system is also contained in the λ-system.

We learn about and how the Borel sigma algebra generated on the topological product of separable spaces is the measure tensor product of the Borel sigma algebras.

We also see that between any two we have induced sigma algebras on the domain and codomain, taking the form of preimages of measurable sets and sets whose preimage is a measurable set.
